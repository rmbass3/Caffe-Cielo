nuovo

(nuò·vo · new)

At the Cielo, we strive to bring our customers only the best coffee and pastries. All ingredients are local, and non-GMO. Pastries are always made fresh daily. Coffee is ground in-house, and brewed using our state-of-the-art machines.

A wide selection of

bevande

(bev·on·dey · drinks)

Creating the perfect drink for each and every customer is our goal. Choose from a wide selection of coffee types, and then customize it however you'd like. We'd be happy to make your drink iced, or put in additions such as caramel or cream.

Our Story

Our founder, Aurelio Pisani came to the U.S in 1900 and founded a small coffee shop. That same coffee shop has grown to become Caffè Cielo. Over the years, we've grown to accomodate our customers by expanding our menu and creating a comfortable space to enjoy coffee and pastries.

Our Staff

The staff at the Cielo is like family. We constantly strive to make our work environment rewarding and enjoyable. If you are interested in joining us, please contact us via email.
